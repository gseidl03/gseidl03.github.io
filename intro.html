How to make Bolognese

*This project is purely for educational purposes, all credit goes to Binging with Babish*

Bolognese is a traditional italian meat sauce that, despite its name, does not originiate from Bologna. Historians believe the dish originated in Imola, a city that sits just west of Bologna, and is home to the earliest documented rag√π sauce, dating from the end of the 18th century. Wherever it originates from, this dish is guaranteed to have your mouth-watering and is well worth its relatively lengthy cooking time. It is recommended to make everything from scratch, but chicken stock and pasta can be replaced with store bought options to save a bit of time in the kitchen.
